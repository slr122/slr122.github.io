Reflection

The purpose of this mini project was to polish the previous pages that I had completed and make them look more professional. In order to begin the process, I downloaded the free trial of Adobe Dreamweaver and initially worked on finiding a color combination that I felt was appropriate. I decided upon a sky blue background mixed with splahes of pale pink text boxes and dark blue text. From here, I started playing around with different font styles, and ended up deciding upon a few differnt styles that I felt worked well for section headings and also paragraph text.

Once I changed the font types on each page, I then added a navigation bar to each of the pages to make it easy for the user to go back and forth between projects. After the navigation bars were added, I then worked on making modifactions to each individual page. For Mini Project 1, I changed ordered list that I originally had to a table, and then added pictures to correspond to each section in my "favorties" table. In Mini Project 2,3 and 4, I worked on making sure that the headings and tables in each section were uniform and aligned to MP1 . Finally, I created my Mini Project 5 page and adjusted the tables and fonts to coincide with other pages.

Overall, I thought that Dreamweaver was very user-friendly, and I enjoyed working with it to modify my previous project pages. It definitely took some time to familiarize myself with the program; however, once I got used to the different featues of the program, it made editing my pages rather simplistic. I do with that Adobe allowed a longer free trial like some of their other software, because it would be nice to be able to use features in other projects moving forward. I am happy with the results of my pages after taking the time to work with Dreamweaver and edit them, and I feel as though they look much more polished and professional.
